Mission Releases

Mission

Our mission is to build AI models and SDKs which will allow mobile developers to integrate intelligent child-safeguarding solutions running on-device (edge).

Recent advances in AI and mobile hardware provide us with an unprecedented opportunity to accomplish this goal without compromising privacy.

Currently, we are focused on the following three key areas:

Detecting harmful images containing nudity, violence and horror.

Real-time harmful video scene detection and blocking.

Identifying cyberbullying and child abuse in conversations.

We believe that as these solutions mature, they will make their way into the core of operating systems and become ubiquitous.

Read more in our blog post: How Artificial Intelligence Can Help Protect Children.

Releases

Open-source AI models and software libraries for detecting harmful content.

Geacc Model

General Audience Content Classifier (Geacc) is a pre-trained deep neural network model for classifying images which are suitable for general audiences with a particular focus on children and adolescents below the age of 12.

Geacc Chrome Extension

Google Chrome extension which uses Geacc pre-trained model and TensorFlow.js to detect explicit images in browser.



Geacc TensorFlow.js Demo

Demo web app which uses Geacc pre-trained model and TensorFlow.js to detect explicit images.
